About This Holiday Home

This 4-bedroom beachfront property in Nyali offers a luxurious and comfortable living experience with stunning sea views. It features a fully equipped kitchen, air conditioning, ceiling fans, and smart TV in each room. All rooms are ensuite, and the property includes amenities such as a lift, pool, gym, and backup generator. It is located just 2 minutes from Naivas Center and 2-7 minutes from major hotels like Voyager. Additional services include housekeeping, free parking, and the option for a chef at an extra cost. The property also offers convenient Wi-Fi and hot water for a perfect stay.
